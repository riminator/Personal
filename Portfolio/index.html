F-18 Remastered

Objectives

Redesign and refine a high-fidelity CAD model of the F-18 to improve geometric accuracy, surface continuity, and subsystem realism while maintaining manufacturable and structurally plausible aircraft features.

Technical Details

  • Rebuilt the aircraft geometry using parametric surface and solid modeling to achieve accurate fuselage curvature and wing–body blending
  • Modeled major aerodynamic components including wings, stabilators, vertical tails, and engine inlets with attention to realistic proportions
  • Implemented feature-based design for control surfaces and structural elements to allow iterative refinement
  • Ensured clean topology and surface continuity suitable for rendering, simulation prep, or future CFD meshing

Skills & Tools

  • CAD (SolidWorks/Fusion360)
  • Parametric Modeling
  • Aircraft Geometry & Layout
  • Aerospace Design Fundamentals

Cube Satellite Model

Objectives

Design a CubeSat platform integrating a compact liquid propulsion system to enable controlled orbital maneuvering, attitude adjustments, and mission profile flexibility while maintaining mass, volume, and power constraints inherent to CubeSat architectures.

Technical Details

  • Developed a subsystem-level CAD architecture integrating propulsion, structure, avionics volume, and payload space within CubeSat form-factor constraints
  • Designed a liquid propulsion layout including propellant tanks, feed lines, valves, and thruster placement with attention to mass distribution and center-of-mass stability
  • Allocated internal volumes to support power, communication, and control subsystems while preserving modularity for mission-specific payloads
  • Structured the design to support multiple mission modes, including Earth-pointing configurations, maneuvering profiles, and attitude control operations

Skills & Tools

  • CubeSat Systems Engineering
  • Liquid Propulsion Architecture
  • Spacecraft CAD Design
  • CAD (Fusion 360 / SolidWorks)
